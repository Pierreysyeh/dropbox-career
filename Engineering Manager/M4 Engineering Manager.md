 # M4 Engineering Manager

**_I employ a robust managerial toolbox to independently lead a team of engineers delivering direct business impact_**

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Scope**<br><br>_Area of ownership and level of autonomy / ambiguity_ | **Collaborative** **Reach**<br><br>_Organizational reach and extent of influence_ | **Impact Levers**<br><br>_Organizational levers typically exercised to achieve business impact_ |
| * I independently lead my team to deliver business impact through successfully driving results on projects in support of semi-annual/annual goals.<br><br>* I am increasingly able to independently identify and tackle projects that solve open-ended, ambiguous problems that are often beyond the scope of my immediate team. | * I am effective at influencing outcomes across multiple teams and with cross-functional partners, often requiring conflict resolution and managing ambiguity. | * I autonomously guide my team to the sustainable delivery of business impact through my mastery of execution and people management.<br><br>* I am increasingly able to achieve impact through influence, planning strategically, problem solving and effective decision making.<br><br>* I cultivate a healthy environment of growth and support that empowers my teams to consistently deliver at a high level of impact. |

| **Responsibility** | **Key Behaviors** |
| **Impact** | * I identify new opportunities for my team to execute that have org/company-wide impact <br><br>* I refocus my team's efforts when results aren’t moving the needle for our business/team goals or serving the needs of customers in a meaningful way<br><br>* I know which levers to pull to drive meaningful results and understand the wider, cross-functional implications of my team's work<br><br>* I am responsible for ensuring that results do not come at the expense of long term growth, retention, or psychological safety<br><br>* My team is delivering results across multiple impactful projects while demonstrating a very high standard of execution |
| **Ownership** | * I have a sense of responsibility and obligation to act on opportunities I see across my org<br><br>* I create an environment where my teams can learn from failures to drive better business outcomes<br><br>* I unblock myself and my team by assessing and eliminating the root cause |
| **Decision Making** | * I make informed decisions by consulting the right stakeholders and balancing details with the big picture<br><br>* I'm able to reach the right decision despite conflicting perspectives and act thoughtfully and decisively |
| **Operational Excellence** | * I set goals and objectives at the right altitude and hold my team accountable to them<br><br>* I run efficient processes, while minimizing unnecessary work about work. I ensure that appropriate information is disseminated within the team.<br><br>* I drive operational cadences that get to the right outcomes<br><br>* I build healthy partnerships with stakeholders and teams with whom my teams have long-lived relationships |

| **Innovation** | * I champion bold, creative, and productive approaches for my team and lead them to generate solutions that aim to deliver better results<br><br>* I set audacious goals for my team, encourage them to take risks, and share lessons learned<br><br>* I create psychological safety for my team to promote experimentation and learning |
| **Strategy** | * I am  increasingly thinking about how my team fits within the broader engineering organization and I make tradeoffs that optimize for the overall organization rather than locally for my team<br><br>* I define the vision for my teams in alignment with company-wide priorities and inspire my team and cross-discipline stakeholders to work toward that vision<br><br>* I resolve misalignments with stakeholder teams and cross-discipline partners with differing goals<br><br>* I am independently planning for and articulating  goals over increasingly longer time horizons, even in the face of material ambiguity |

| **Personal Growth** | * I proactively ask for feedback from those I work with and have self-awareness about my strengths and areas for development<br><br>* I drive discussions with my manager about aspirational goals and seek out opportunities to learn and grow<br><br>* I invest time in being a better manager and leader |
| **Hiring** | * I attract and hire a diverse and talented team with a broad range of experiences and strengths<br><br>* I am actively involved in interviewing and assessing candidates to help us build a diverse and talented team. In addition, I ensure my team is calibrated and conducting interviews.<br><br>* I build strong relationships with candidates and can represent Dropbox's mission, strategy, and culture throughout the interview process  <br><br>* I have a strong pulse on my team's engagement and a proactive retention strategy |
| **Talent Development** | * I foster the career growth of my team members by taking into account their career goals and looking for ways to provide meaningful development experiences based on their skills and interest. I give opportunities to work on next-level assignments that demonstrate readiness for promotion when appropriate<br><br>* I hold my team accountable for performance expectations and take different approaches to motivating high performers and turning around low performers  <br><br>* I can guide and grow senior ICs, helping them advance their careers by finding highly-leveraged roles for them within the organization, not just my team |
| **Organizational planning** | * I structure my team so the right people are in the right roles with clearly defined responsibilities. I independently ensure projects are staffed appropriately to set them up for success, placing my strongest technical leaders where they are the most needed and impactful.<br><br>* I can independently plan for the headcount  and recruiting needs of my team |
